# About DIVORCE CRUSHER

We're dads who've been through the system. We know what it's like to sit in a courtroom wondering if you'll ever see your kids again. We know what it's like to feel like the entire legal system is designed to take everything from you.

We built DIVORCE CRUSHER because nobody else was telling dads the truth. Not the lawyers who bill you $400/hour to lose. Not the self-help books written by people who've never set foot in a courtroom. Not the government websites that read like they were written by the people trying to take your kids.

## What We Do

We read court decisions. Thousands of them. Real Canadian cases where fathers fought and won. We break them down into plain language and show you exactly what worked.

Every article on this site cites real cases from real courts. No made-up scenarios. No hypotheticals. No bullshit.

## What We're Building

We're developing an interactive platform where dads can get case-law-grounded answers to their specific situations. Powered by AI, backed by thousands of real Canadian court decisions.

**Your feedback shapes what we build.** Tell us what you're fighting. Tell us what you need. We're building this for YOU.

## What We Are NOT

We are not lawyers. We are not your lawyers. We do not give legal advice. We document publicly available court decisions and show you what happened when other dads were in your situation. What you do with that information is your business.
