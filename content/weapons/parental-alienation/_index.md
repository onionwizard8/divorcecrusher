# PARENTAL ALIENATION — THE BATTLEFIELD

Parental alienation is one of the most weaponized concepts in family court. It cuts both ways:

- **She's alienating your kids against you** — and you need to prove it
- **She's accusing YOU of alienation** — and you need to destroy the claim

Both scenarios are documented in Canadian case law. Both have been won by fathers who knew the law and brought the evidence.

The leading case is **Williamson v. Williamson, 2016 BCCA 87** — cited in 100+ Canadian court decisions. Every dad in an alienation fight needs to know this case cold.
